Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will ADD a specific amount of points to your total.

You win the game by matching your total to the random number. If your total score goes above the random number, try again.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
